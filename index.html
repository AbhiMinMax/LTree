Understanding What You Can Control

Life is often brutal and unfair. External factors beyond your control will frequently disappoint you. This app focuses solely on what remains within your power: your attention, intention, and mindfulness.

We'll ask you simple questions about fundamental choices, then show you two types of predictions:

  • Abstract Aspects: Your internal strength and resilience (highly predictable)
  • Concrete Outcomes: External results like money, respect, relationships (highly unpredictable)
